Meet Avery

Born and raised in Columbus, Ohio, Avery has worked and excelled in the hospitality and events industry for over a decade. As a graduate of the Culinary Institute of Fort Lauderdale, she began her career in events at the Milo Island Resort as well as Marketing Director for Holy City Hospitality. Avery was recruited by Willowbrook Hospitality Group as the Director of Hospitality + Events to launch its inaugural venue Upstairs at Midtown, on to opening the awarded Gadsden House, Maybank Catering and Willowbrook Beverage Company. Avery decided it was time to get back to her design roots and knew event planning & design is where she always wanted to end up. From the planning stages to execution, Avery will provide each and every client with individualized service to ensure success. Her personalized planning experience creates events that guest will be swooning over for years to come.
