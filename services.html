Services

We offer three packages guaranteed to exceed your wedding day expectations. From bridal gown appointments to booking your fights to breathtaking honeymoon locations, we make the arrangements. All you have to do is show up!

Diamond Package

This package includes design, planning and coordinating services for your full wedding week! This is the package for you if you would like help with spa days, golf outings, welcome parties, the wedding (of course), after-parties and a farewell brunch - or any combination of these events!

Platinum Package

This package includes design, planning and coordinating services for the full wedding day! This package is for you if you are looking for someone to help you bring your dreams to life. Not only do we help design the wedding day so everything from your invitations to your guest's entertainment scream you as a couple but we are there the day of the wedding coordinating all the vendors and doing all the set up!

Gold Package

This package includes month of coordination. If you just need someone to help pull together the final details and need someone onsite the day of the wedding for set-up and coordination this is the package for you!
